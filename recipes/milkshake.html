Stawberry Milkshake

Description

This strawberry milkshake is thick, creamy, and everything that you would hope a milkshake would be! Made with delicious strawberries, this classic dessert is sure to be loved by all!

Ingredients

  • 250 ml milk
  • 2.5 Scoops of vanilla ice cream
  • 2 tables spoon of yogurt
  • 1 cup of mixed frozen berries/stawberries
  • 1.5 scoops of protein powder
  • 1 bannana
  • 30g Oats

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Put everything in a blender
  3. Mix for 1 minute
  4. Pour into cup and enjoy!
